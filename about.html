Crypton Inc.

Crypton Inc is a multi-national company based in the capital city of the Nigerian Republic, Lagos, currently recognised as the leading company by sales in the Sofware Design Industry. It's best-known product is called the 'Endenigma Machine' built in the 19th century to help Nigerian soldiers communicate encoded information to each other without the Zambian Army understanding. The company mostly specialises in the development of various softwares of different uses mostly the supply a high level of demand and its meticulous standard of design has spanned its horizon over many countries.

History

Founded in 1856, by brilliant mastermind Tomisin Ogunnubi, Crypton Inc. started out as Softwares and Co just as an approach to briefly explain the aim of the company. Due to her limited resources she started out with a team of five consisting of two of her friends and other family members in her garage back at home. Very soon, her work began to sell and many companies began to invest and five years down the line, The Nigerian Army reached out to the company which led to the production of the 'Endenigma machine'
